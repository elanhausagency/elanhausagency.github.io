Our Methodology

A Process Built
on Precision

Every Elan Haus engagement follows a four-phase methodology developed over 12 years. It's rigorous, transparent, and designed to deliver results — every single time.

01
Phase One

Discovery & Strategy

Every great campaign begins with deep understanding. Our strategists immerse themselves in your brand — your history, values, visual identity, target audience, and competitive landscape.

We conduct a thorough brand audit, define campaign objectives, and establish KPIs that guide everything that follows. We also identify the creator archetypes most likely to resonate with your audience.

Deliverables
  • Brand Audit Report
  • Campaign Brief
  • KPI Framework
  • Creator Archetype Profile
  • Competitive Overview
02
Phase Two

Creator Curation

Using our proprietary matching methodology, we identify and vet a curated shortlist of creators. We analyse audience demographics, engagement quality, content aesthetics, brand safety, and cultural alignment.

Every creator is presented with a detailed profile including audience breakdown, engagement rates, past brand work, and a qualitative fit assessment. You choose — we handle the rest.

Deliverables
  • Creator Shortlist (3–10)
  • Audience Analytics Reports
  • Engagement Quality Scores
  • Brand Safety Assessment
  • Collaboration Format Rec.
03
Phase Three

Campaign Execution

Once creators are confirmed, we manage the entire lifecycle. We negotiate contracts, prepare detailed creative briefs, coordinate content review rounds, and manage the publishing schedule across all platforms.

We act as the bridge between brand and creator — ensuring the brief is followed while protecting creative freedom, the ingredient that makes influencer content actually perform.

Deliverables
  • Signed Contracts
  • Creative Briefs
  • Content Review & Approval
  • Publishing Schedule
  • Live Campaign Dashboard
04
Phase Four

Amplification & Reporting

Post-publish, we monitor performance in real time and identify opportunities to amplify top-performing content through paid boosting, cross-platform repurposing, and earned media outreach.

Every campaign concludes with a comprehensive post-campaign report covering reach, engagement, earned media value, sentiment analysis, and strategic recommendations for next time.

Deliverables
  • Performance Report
  • EMV Calculation
  • Sentiment Analysis
  • Content Rights Summary
  • Future Strategy Recs.
